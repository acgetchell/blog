This page collects a small set of PDFs of possible interest.

- [Agent-based Modeling](/papers/Agent-based-Modeling.pdf) — An introduction to agent-based modeling, with an emphasis on how to build and reason about simple ABMs.
- [CDT survey](/papers/cdt-survey.pdf) — A survey-style overview of causal dynamical triangulations and the ideas that motivate the approach.
- [Newtonian derivation (CDT)](/papers/newtonian-derivation-cdt.pdf) — A short note working through a Newtonian-style derivation in the context of causal dynamical triangulations.
- [OAUP wormholes](/papers/oaup-wormholes.pdf) — A brief paper exploring wormhole-related ideas and results in the [Orion's Arm Universe Project](https://www.orionsarm.com) setting.
- [Traversable Lorentzian Wormholes (Overview)](/papers/TraversableLorentzianWormholes-Overview.pdf) — A high-level overview of traversable Lorentzian wormholes and the main ideas behind the work.
